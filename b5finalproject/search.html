Scroll through to find volunteer opportunities!

Zoouniverse

Zooniverse is a virtual platform that allows volunteers from around the world to participate in citizen science projects.

Red Cross

Red Cross is global humanitarian organization that provides aid to people and regions that are in need. They invite organizations to become a part of their mission!

WhyHunger is a non-profit organization that is working to end hunger and poverty.

The Trevor Project

The Trevor Project is a national organization that provides crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and questioning youth.

UpChieve

UpChieve is an edtech non-profit organization that provide assistance to ow-income high school students, by allowing them to connect to on-demand live tutors and counselors at any time.

Amnesty Decoders

Amnesty Decoders is a platform where digital volunteers around the world utilize technical devices to help researchers evaluate large databanks of social media messages, images, documents and other information.

Crisis Text Line

Crisis Text Line is a not-for-profit organization that provides text-based mental health support and crisis intervention by training a community of volunteers to support people in moments of need.

Be My Eyes

Be My Eyes is an app that connects low-vision and blind people with sighted volunteers for virtual assistance through video calls.

United Nation Volunteers

United Nations Volunteer is a program that supports peace, development, and human rights in communities most in need. By serving in the program volunteers can enact positive change and lead to a brighter future!

Catch A Fire

CatchAFire matches volunteers with non-profits in order to help them increase their capacity and reach their mission.

Smithsonian Digital Volunteers

Smithsonian Digital Volunteer allows volunteers to take on important assignments to expand access to its massive collections, and contribute to a variety of research programs.

Project Gutenberg

Project Gutenberg is a volunteer effort to digitize, archive, and distribute literary works.
